Hiding your BIND version

To hide the version of bind that your are running when someone uses the following dig command:
dig @ns1.example.com version.bind txt chaos

Add the following configuration setting in the options section of your /etc/named.conf as shown below:

options {
    version "Unknown";
};

Then restart the server.
