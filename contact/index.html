Mark Terry

Data Platform Technical Lead in Kent, UK

Mark Terry is a tech lead at Holiday Extras, the UK's market leader in travel add-ons. He has more than 22 years’ experience, from linux systems, databases and security, to e-commerce, and web tech – including running his own web-hosting startup. Mark currently leads a team developing Holiday Extras’ real-time streaming and data-processing platform, utilising his passion for all things data and its vital role in unlocking business potential. Based in sunny Kent, UK, his weekends are spent enjoying a combination of mountain biking and thinking of reasons for deploying yet another Raspberry Pi around the house.
